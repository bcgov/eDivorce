How long does it take to get a file number?

The time depends on how many filings are received at the court registry you are filing with. Normally, you will receive a file number within a week of submitting online. If you do not receive a file number, you can send an email to CSO Support Helpdesk. Please provide the package number and registry location you submitted your documents to and they will be able to help.
