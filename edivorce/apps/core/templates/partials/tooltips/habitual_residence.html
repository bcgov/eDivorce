  • The place where a person resides in the habitual course of his or her day to day
  • You do not lose your habitual residence in a place when you leave for a temporary purpose (e.g. go to school in another province)

Not an Habitual Residence
  • If you have travelled to another place to live and work indefinitely (even though you ultimately intend to return to the prior home)

A detailed explanation of the meaning of habitual residence can be found on the Department of Justice website.
