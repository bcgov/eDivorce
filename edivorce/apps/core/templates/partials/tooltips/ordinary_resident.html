  • The place where a person resides in the ordinary course of his or her day to day
  • You do not lose your ordinary residence in a place when you leave for a temporary purpose (e.g. go to school in another province)

Not an Ordinary Residence
  • If you have travelled to another place to live and work indefinitely (even though you ultimately intend to return to the prior home)

A detailed explanation of the meaning of ordinary residence can be found on the Department of Justice website.
